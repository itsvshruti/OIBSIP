LOKMANYA BAL GANGADHAR TILAK

About the Legend

*Keshav Gangadhar Tilak popularly known as Bal Gangadhar Tilak.
he was an Indian nationalist, teacher,journalist, and independence activist. Bal Gangadhar Tilak was the Indian Independence Movement's firstleader.

* He was one of the Lal Bal Pal triumvirate's three members. He was dubbed "The Father of IndianUnrest" by British colonial authorities.He was also given the title of "Lokmanya," which means"accepted as a leader by the people."

*He was dubbed "The Maker of Modern India" by Mahatma Gandhi. BalGangadhar Tilak was a strong radical in Indian consciousness and one of the first and strongestsupporters of Swaraj (self-rule).
